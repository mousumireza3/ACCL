ABOUT US

Amir Construction and Co. has started its journey 10 years ago. It has its head office in Dhaka, Bangladesh. The company deals with different equipment like Excavator, Crane, Bulldozer, Dump Truck, Wheel Loader and Container....

WHAT WE DO

IMPORT

We import both brand new and recondition equipment from Japan and Korea. Besides, we import on demand equipment.

SALE

We have a wide range of equipment collection of almost all brands and models for Sales.

RENT

We have equipment like excavator, crane, bulldozer, wheel loader, dump truck, mixer, container etc available for rent.

MAINTENANCE

We have yard at Ashulia, Savar for selling equipment parts, lubricants and servicing of equipment along with yard rent.
